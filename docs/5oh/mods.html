List of Included Mods

Mod Loader​

  • Darktide Mod Loader - Required for all mods and the Darktide Mod Framework.
  • Darktide Mod Framework - Required for all mods.

Load Order​

  • Auto Mod Loading and Ordering - Automatically orders and loads valid mods without requiring mod_load_order.txt

Core Mods​

  • AFK - Delays the AFK check.
  • Animation Events - Required mod for Scoreboard.
  • Archivum Messelina - Adds a filter/search to the penances in the Penance screen.
  • Better Hud Info View - Adjusts HUD elements of the tactical overlay to fit better.
  • Claim All Penances - Claim all reward from penances at once and all tier rewards.
  • Click2Play - In the character select screen, simply re-select your currently-selected character to start playing as them.
  • Correct Mission Voting Info - When voting for a mission, shows correct auric info and total rewards.
  • Crosshairs Fix - Fixes crosshairs to be actually representative of spread, taking your FOV into account.
  • Custom HUD - Allows the user to move various HUD elements, with optional grid and snapping. Soft requirement for Specials Tracker.
  • Default To Highest Blessing Tier - Automatically switch to the highest unlocked blessing tier in Re-Bless page.
  • Display Ping (Latency) - Adds ping text to the HUD with a lot of customization options.
  • DynamicCrosshair - Changes your crosshair's color when aiming over an enemy, ally, or barrel and dynamically adjusts its position in 3rd person.
  • Enhanced Descriptions - Enhanced Descriptions, highlighted numbers and keywords for Weapon/Curios Blessings and Perks, and Talents.
  • Equipped Icon Plus - Show equipped ☑ icons for items in other loadouts and customize icons color.
  • For the Emperor - Enhanced Comms Wheel - Add "Yes", "No" and "Need Help" to comms wheel with voicelines and world markers, customize buttons and replace "That's Mine" with a refreshed callout when retagging.
  • Gas Outline - Fixes an issue where outlines will disappear while standing in Toxic Gas.
  • Healthbars - Show healthbars from the Psykanium in regular game modes, togglable by enemy class.
  • Killfeed Improvements - Deduplicate feed items and filter by type.
  • LessAnnoyingPing - Replace Annoying Ping/Tag Sound with the less annoying Eye Marker Sound, additional options to reduce spam and griefing from other players.
  • loadinglore - This mod will overwrite the default loading screen messages to instead include a bunch of helpful tips sourced from the community.
  • Log Me In - Automatically hits space for you at the title screen and automatically selects your current character.
  • Markers Improved All-in-One - An all-in-one bundle of the "Markers" series of mods aiming at reworking all markers in the game.
  • MarkPreview - Detailed Marks - See the weapon details when switching marks.
  • MasterItems Community Patch - Required for Character Cosmetics View Improved, Extended Weapon Customization, Weapon Cosmetics View Improved.
  • Merged Talent Stats - Merge Operative Modifier stats in the talents Full Build popup.
  • Murmur Hashdt - Required mod
  • Numeric UI - Adds more visual information to the HUD.
  • Psykhanium Inv Hotkey - You can open inventory in Psykhanium by pressing hotkey 'I'.
  • Recolor Boss Health Bars - Show more than two boss health bars at once, and change the color of boss health bars to differentiate different boss units / weakened monsters.
  • Show current penance stats - Show the current stats for penances that can reset, instead of the highest record.
  • Stimms Pickup Icon - Set corresponding color for stimms HUD icons.
  • What The Localization - A community-driven localization fix framework and debug tool.

Optional But Recommended​

  • BornReady - Configurably automate readying up when joining a lobby, skipping the end-of-mission screen, accepting mission prompts, and accepting/declining party invites and join requests.
  • Chat Block - Automatically hold block while chatting, opening the Steam overlay, or while the game is not focused.
  • Commodore's Vestures Improved - Adds a bunch of QoL features to the Commodore's Vestures (premium) shop. Including letting you preview bundles and showing items directly on your characters, without having to re-enter the store as the correct class.
  • Danger Zone - Shows the range for area of effect hazards like fire, explosions, Daemonhost aggro, and more. Comes with customizable colours and display options.
  • Emperor's Guidance - Allows for per-enemy target filtering for Psyker's Brain Burst. Filters can be set for primary and secondary attacks.
  • Empower Until Limit - Keeps empowering the weapon until reaching its limit.
  • FullAuto - Make all possible ranged weapons fully automatic, either by default or with a keypress. The mod will account for your weapon's fire rate and your current attack speed.
  • Guarantee Ability Activation - Guarantee combat ability activation when player try to activate them in middle of animation.
  • Guarantee Special Action - Guarantee special action and reload action when the player try to perform them in the middle of certain animations.
  • Guarantee Weapon Swap - Guarantee weapon swapping when player try to swap weapon in middle of animation
  • Hub Hotkeys - Hotkeys for main hub's menus.
  • Instant Ability - Actives your ability the moment you press the button, rather than having it wait for you to release the button.
  • KeepSwinging - Adds keybinds to spam light melee attacks for you.
  • Kill Tracker - Tracks all mission kills and kill combos on the HUD. Useful for comparing purging efficiency between weapons.
  • MissionBrief - Shows mission details in the loading screen.
  • Modular Menu Buttons - Allows to customize the buttons displayed in the esc menu.
  • More Characters and Loadouts - Allow the creation of up to 8 characters and 12 loadouts per character.
  • Nothing New - Will clean up all 'new item' notification dots on the UI and can suppress the 'New Penance' top right notification in the Mourningstar
  • Pause Vision - Makes the pause & options menu more transparent.
  • Penance View Improved - Improves the penance screen by adding "recently completed" penances, more details about each penance and sub-penance, ability to view rewards on your operatives and more!
  • Quick-Inject Stimms - Adds a button to switch to your Stimm and instantly inject. (Also known as PanicStimm)
  • Quick Chat - Sends preset messages with hotkeys or automatically when certain events are triggered.
  • Quick Look Card - Check weapon stats in item list quickly.
  • Red Weapons at Home - Display weapons as red when they reach their maximum potential of base stats
  • Reorder Characters - Allows you to reorder your characters on the character select screen.
  • Reveal Blessings - Reveal all blessings available for the weapon in Re-Bless screen.
  • Rolling Steel Timer Improved - Adds a new countdown timer alongside the progress bar in the new Rolling Steel map. (Also known as train_timer)
  • Scanner Darkly - Adjusts the caption transparency while using the Auspex so it doesn't get in the way.
  • Share Talents - Copy, paste and share talents builds.
  • Show Crit Chance - Display the critical strike chance of active weapon in real time.
  • Show Weapons In Lobby - Press to Swap Weapon in the Lobby (Also known as ShowEquippedInLobby)
  • Show Insignias - Shows player insignias during missions.
  • Show Me Real Weapon Stats - Shows real weapon stats with a maximum value of 80.
  • Smaller Talent Tree - Allow you to squash the talent trees, changes are taken into effect after leaving the talent tab or changing loadout twice.
  • SpecialsTracker - Allows the tracking of specific special/monster units through notifications and/or a permanent HUD element.
  • Stimmfo - Adds a text description to the stimms in game to show what effects it will have.
  • Talent Tree UX Improvements - Remove a talent and all subsequent talents in one right-click, and swap talents without having to redo what's below them with a double-click (Also known as TalentRefundBelow)
  • Volley Fire Timer - See a timer showing how long you have to get your next kill to chain the veteran sharpshooters volley fire ability.
  • Zealot Fire Particle Swap - Replaces the fire lingering particles of the fire grenade to other particles for fun and accessibility. (Also known as BetterFireGrenade)

Optional​

  • BetterDogTargets - Recolor dog target ping outlines
  • Collectible Finder - Displays a notification when collectibles are nearby.
  • Custom Flames - Customize the flamer and flamestaff by choosing different styles and colors.
  • Decode Helper - Shows you all the targets for the decode symbols minigame.
  • Default to Strike Team Chat - Set default chat channel to Strike Team.
  • Direct to Hadron - Go to the crafting menu from inventory - Provides a hotkey to directly access the crafting menu for the selected item from inventory
  • Dog Whistle - Target filtration for the Arbitrator Cyber-Mastiff, with optional auto-tag.
  • Inventory Inspect - Conveniently inspect your party members from the inventory screen.
  • Item Sorting - Add extra item sorting options and allow toggling vanilla sorting methods
  • Filter Sacrifice - Displays only the same weapon type in the sacrifice menu.
  • GoToMastery - Adds a "Mastery" button to the inventory screen and re-bless menu. Adds buttons in the inventory to go to the crafting menu.
  • LessAnnoyingHud - Tiny little mod to let you adjust the "brightness" of the main HUD colors. Requires restart for some HUD elements to take effect.
  • Name It - Rename Weapons and Curios - Changes weapons/curios name as you wish.
  • Penances for the Mission - Displays available penances for the selected mission in the Mission Terminal.
  • PewPew - Change your ranged weapon's firing sound and/or the projectile it shoots.
  • Quickest Play - Make it easier to (re)start quickplay matchmaking.
  • Scan Helper - Show all targets that need to be scanned.
  • Simple Speed Meter - Adds a simple player speed meter widget.
  • Sort Mastery - Sorts Brunt's Armory and Mastery lists to keep similar items together. (Also known as SortBrunt)
  • Team Outline Colours - Set teammate outline colours to match their icon. (Also known as CareerColourOutlines)
  • ToggleHUD - Allows turning the game UI on and off.
  • Trauma Outlines - Highlights enemies within Voidblast radius
  • Tree Helper - This mod shows you the correct scan targets to choose for the Hab Dreyko tree event.
  • True Level - Reads recorded exp earned after you hit the max level, and displays the actual level you can reach.
  • UngaBunga - A mod to spam heavy attacks as fast as possible.
  • Valkyrie Blitz-Ingress - Reduce time in the Valkyrie when joining an in-progress mission by skipping the mission briefing.
  • Weapon FOV - Change the FOV of weapons and the zoom of ranged weapons.

Weapon Customization​

  • Extended Weapon Customization - Weapon customization, flashlights, sights, scopes, equipment on back.
  • The Syndonai Edits - An Extended Weapons Customization Plugin - This plugin adds a selection of kit-bashed options to the weapon customization mod, many influenced by items from WH40K and other sci-fi universes.
  • Weapon Customization MT Plugin - Adds a large selection of weapon parts to use.
  • Ostracized without Objection (OwO) - Extended Weapon Customization Plugin - Weapon Customization plugin adding various parts, typically inspired by historical weapons.
note

Weapon Customization can cause game instability, frame drops, and crashes. Comes disabled. All plugins require the main mod enabled and may require each other. Make sure you check the dependencies on the modpage.

Waiting for Updates​

  • How Did I Get That (Unlock Requirements Improved) - Shows details on how you unlocked/can unlock all cosmetics, including penance rewards, commissary items and Hestia's blessing rewards.
  • Character Cosmetics View Improved - Displays all premium cosmetics available through Commodore's Vestures in the character cosmetics screen, and allows you to preview them, and go directly to the items in the store (If they are in the current rotation).
  • Scoreboard - Shows a scoreboard after missions and additional messages for certain actions during gameplay.
  • Scoreboard Enhanced Defense Plugin - Provides additional defensive stats to the Scoreboard.
  • Weapon Cosmetics View Improved - Lets you view locked weapon cosmetics such as skins and trinkets (including premium items), just like the character cosmetic screen.
  • Coherency Keeper - Displays a directional marker when a player leaves your coherency.
